# startup-finance

Use this skill for startup finance, metrics, and market sizing work.

## Workflow

1. Identify company type, stage, audience, and decision being supported.
2. Choose the relevant reference: metrics, financial model, or market sizing.
3. Ask for missing assumptions before inventing precise numbers.
4. Produce assumptions, formulas, scenario outputs, and caveats.
