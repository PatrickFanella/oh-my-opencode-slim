# Competitor skill boundaries

Keep these separate because they happen at different stages and produce different artifacts.

- `competitor-profiling` researches competitors from URLs and produces factual dossiers.
- `competitor-alternatives` turns known competitors/profiles into comparison, alternative, battle-card, or SEO pages.
- `competitive-landscape` reasons at market/strategy level: positioning, forces, differentiation, and investment/strategy decisions.

Do not merge unless the catalog needs one broad marketing-generalist skill. Separate skills reduce context load and avoid mixing research, content production, and strategy judgment.
